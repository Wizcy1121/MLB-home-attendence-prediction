## --》Pre-Season Attendance Prediction Model
1. Data features
Team performance: 
Home Team/Visiting Team:
  · Season end rank,
  · Season end Win/Loss ratio,
  · Total # of runs scored/allowed
2. Players
Home Team/Visiting Team:
  · # of all star players in team,
  · # of top 10 players in team,
  · Previous season MVP in team
3. Calendar
  · Year, month,
  · Weekday number, 
  · Holidays
